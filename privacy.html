Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to Ustory. We take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how Ustory (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you use our application. By using Ustory, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Core Principle: Local-First & Data Minimization

Ustory is built around privacy protection and processes your data on your device whenever possible. We do not upload your original photos, and we do not collect personal information for advertising or identity profiling. To provide network-based AI features, verify subscriptions, and prevent abuse, we process a minimal amount of necessary technical information in an anonymized manner, as described below.

2. Photos & On-Device Processing

When you grant access to your system photo library, all core processing (such as scanning, metadata organization, and time/location clustering to build your memory timeline) is performed locally on your device.

  • Generated memory records, contacts, diaries, and tags are stored in your device’s local database.
  • Your original photos are never uploaded to any server by Ustory.

3. Location Lookup (Apple Maps Service)

To display city and landmark names for your memories, Ustory extracts coordinates embedded in your photos and uses the iOS system’s reverse-geocoding API (CLGeocoder) to query Apple’s official map servers. This request is anonymous, contains only coordinate data, and is governed by Apple’s privacy policy.

4. AI Features & Third-Party Providers

Some core services (such as memory summaries, bond analysis, and smart search) rely on AI Large Language Models (LLMs) for text analysis.

  • Text-Only Processing: We never send your original photos to any AI provider. Only necessary, de-identified textual context (such as dates, location names, and diary snippets) is transmitted.
  • Regional Routing: Depending on your network region, service availability, and stability policies, your AI text requests may be sent via secure protocols (such as HTTPS) to third-party AI service providers located in different regions for processing.
  • Data Scope: To control costs and ensure performance, the data sent to the AI may be filtered, truncated, or compressed, so it may not cover all of your memories.

5. Anonymous Server-Side Data & Risk Control

When you actively use network-based AI features, our service proxy layer may process and temporarily record the following anonymous information to provide AI features, verify entitlements, control costs, and prevent abuse:

  • Anonymous subscription/device identifiers generated by the subscription management system.
  • AI feature category, request counts, and token usage.
  • Request frequency and abnormal usage patterns.
  • Approximate country/region resolved from your IP address (used only to select an appropriate AI service endpoint).

This information does not include your real name, phone number, email, or precise location, and is not used to build user profiles or for advertising. It is used solely for service availability, fair use, and security.

6. iCloud & Local Backup

Backups never include your original photo or video files.

If you use local backup or iCloud sync, backups only include memory events, diaries, tags, companions, AI summaries, and system-internal photo reference identifiers (Local Identifiers). Original photos remain managed by your system Photos library and iCloud Photos. If iCloud sync is enabled, your data is synced directly to your personal Apple-protected CloudKit private database, to which the developer has no access.

7. Subscriptions & Anonymous Identifiers (RevenueCat)

To verify your premium entitlements, the app integrates a purchase-validation SDK (RevenueCat). This process transmits only an anonymous transaction token and identifier — excluding your real name or explicit Apple ID credentials — to determine subscription validity, in accordance with App Store anonymization standards.

8. Face ID / Biometric Authentication

The optional Face ID / passcode lock uses the native iOS LocalAuthentication framework and is performed entirely within your device’s Secure Enclave. Ustory never accesses, receives, or stores your biometric data or passcode.

9. Children’s Privacy

Given our local-first architecture, we do not build user profiles in the cloud. The app does not, and cannot, knowingly collect private data from children under the age of 13 (or the age defined by your jurisdiction).

10. Data Retention & Deletion

Your memory data is stored on your device, and you may delete it at any time within the app. Anonymous server-side records (such as usage counts and token usage) are automatically expired and removed after a limited period, and are used only for fair-use and security purposes.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any material changes will be announced within the app or take effect through modification of this page. Your continued use of Ustory after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by email: remember9102@163.com.

隐私政策

最后更新时间：2026 年 6 月

欢迎使用 Ustory。我们高度重视您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了当您使用本应用时，Ustory（以下简称“我们”）如何处理相关信息。使用 Ustory 即表示您同意本政策所述的做法。

1. 核心原则：本地优先与最小化收集

Ustory 以隐私保护为核心，尽可能在本地设备处理数据。我们不会上传您的原始照片，也不会收集用于广告或身份画像的个人信息。为提供联网 AI 功能、校验订阅并防止滥用，我们会以匿名、最小化的方式处理必要的技术信息，详见下文。

2. 照片与本地处理

当您授权访问系统相册后，所有核心处理（如扫描、元数据整理、时间与空间聚类以构建回忆时间线）均在您的本地设备上完成。

  • 生成的回忆记录、联系人、日记与标签均保存在您本机的数据库中。
  • Ustory 绝不会将您的原始照片上传至任何服务器。

3. 地理位置反查（Apple 地图服务）

为向您呈现回忆的城市与景点名称，Ustory 会提取照片内置的坐标，并调用 iOS 系统的地理编码接口（CLGeocoder）向 Apple 官方地图服务器发起反查。该请求匿名且仅包含坐标数据，受 Apple 隐私政策保护。

4. AI 功能与第三方服务提供商

部分核心服务（如回忆总结、羁绊分析、智能搜索）依赖人工智能大语言模型（LLM）进行文本分析。

  • 仅文本处理：我们绝不会向任何 AI 服务商发送您的原始照片，仅传输必要且脱敏的文本上下文（如日期、地点名称、日记片段等）。
  • 区域路由：根据您的网络所在地区、服务可用性与稳定性策略，您的 AI 文本请求可能通过安全传输协议（如 HTTPS）发送至位于不同地区的第三方 AI 服务提供商进行处理。
  • 数据范围：为控制成本与保障性能，发送给 AI 的数据可能经过筛选、截断或压缩，因此可能无法覆盖您的全部回忆记录。

5. 服务端匿名数据与风控

当您主动使用需要联网的 AI 功能时，为提供 AI 功能、验证权益、控制成本并防止滥用，我们的服务代理层可能会处理并临时记录以下匿名信息：

  • 由订阅管理系统生成的匿名订阅/设备标识符。
  • AI 功能的调用类别、调用次数与 token 用量。
  • 请求频率与异常使用特征。
  • 基于 IP 地址粗略解析的国家/地区（仅用于选择合适的 AI 服务节点）。

上述信息不包含您的真实姓名、手机号、邮箱或精确位置，也不会用于构建用户画像或广告用途，仅用于服务可用性、公平使用与安全风控。

6. iCloud 与本地备份

备份绝不包含您的原始照片或视频文件。

若您使用本地备份或 iCloud 同步功能，备份内容仅包含回忆事件、日记、标签、同行人、AI 摘要以及照片的系统内部引用标识（Local Identifiers）。原始照片仍由您的系统相册与 iCloud 照片负责保存。若开启 iCloud 同步，您的数据将直接同步至您个人所属、受 Apple 保护的 CloudKit 私有数据库，开发者对该容器没有任何访问权限。

7. 订阅与匿名标识（RevenueCat）

为校验您的高级权益，本应用集成了内购验证组件（RevenueCat）。该过程仅传递匿名的交易令牌与标识符（不包含您的真实姓名或明确的 Apple ID 凭据）以查询订阅有效性，符合 App Store 的匿名化标准。

8. 面容 ID / 生物识别验证

可选的面容 ID / 密码锁功能完全调用 iOS 原生的 LocalAuthentication 框架，并在您设备的安全隔离区（Secure Enclave）内完成。Ustory 在任何时候都无法获取、读取或留存您的生物特征或锁屏密码。

9. 未成年人隐私保护

基于本地优先的架构，我们不会在云端建立用户画像。本应用不会、也无法故意收集 13 周岁（或您所在法域规定年龄）以下儿童的隐私数据。

10. 数据留存与删除

您的回忆数据保存在您的设备上，您可以随时在应用内删除。服务端的匿名记录（如调用次数、token 用量）将在有限期限后自动过期并删除，且仅用于公平使用与安全目的。

11. 隐私政策的变更

我们可能会不定期更新本隐私政策。任何重大变更都将在应用内发出通知，或通过修改本页面生效。若您在变更后继续使用 Ustory，即视为接受修订后的政策。

12. 联系我们
