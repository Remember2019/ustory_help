Ustory 隐私政策
Privacy Policy

最后更新时间 / Last Updated: June 2026

1. 我们不收集个人数据 (No Data Collection)

Ustory 是一款以隐私为核心的本地化应用。我们不收集、不存储、不分析、不出售您的任何个人信息、照片或回忆数据。
Ustory is a privacy-first app. We do not collect, store, analyze, or sell any of your personal information, photos, or memory data. All processing is done locally on your device.

2. iCloud 同步 (iCloud Syncing)

如果您开启云端备份，数据将加密上传至您个人的 iCloud 账户中。开发者无法查看或获取这些数据。
If you enable cloud backup, your data is securely and privately synced directly to your personal Apple iCloud. The developer has absolutely no access to this data.

3. Face ID 与面容数据 (Face ID & Biometrics)

应用使用的 Face ID 验证完全在设备的安全隔离区内进行，应用绝不会获取您的面部数据。
Face ID authentication happens entirely within your device's Secure Enclave. The app never accesses or stores your biometric data.
